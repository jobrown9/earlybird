Our top 10 Activities in and around Provence

 

 
 

 

 

 

 

Discovering secluded swimming spots and hidden beaches

 

The Cote d'Azur offers some amazing beaches and hidden coves for swimming, sunbathing or snorkeling.

 

Where to go: The Calanque de Casis. These are rocky inlets that don't offer beaches but patches of rock to lie about on and incredible waters to go snorkeling.

 

Cycing along the many routes and tracks

 

Cycling around the region is extremely popular, and can be one of the best ways to get to know some of the villages. There are a number of even track that are perfectly suited to cycling as well as long walks.

Where to go:

 

The old railway tracks that run between Salernes and Sillans la Cascade - ideal for a casual day out with the family. This track extends for over 20 kms

 

 

Wine tasting in one of the many local vineyards

 

There are many spots for wine tasting due to the fact that the whole region is perfectly suited to growing vines.

Where to go:

 

The Cote de Provence Sainte-Victoire wine tour just outside Aix-en-Provence. Run by provencewinetours.com

 

 

 

 

Sailing - on the Lac Saint Croix or along the coast

 

Go up to the Lac Saint Croix and rent out a sailing boat for a couple hours

Where to go:

 

Just outside...

 

 

 

 

 

 

Canoeing & white water rafting

 

Canoeing along refreshing and peaceful stretches of the Argent river can be enjoyed in a number of places. A whole range of difficulty levels offers a fun day out for the family.

 

Where to go:

 

The Gorge du Verdon offers exeptional rapids and scenery

 

 

 

 

 

 

Paragliding

 

Some of the best paragliding is to be had up at Saint Andre les Alpes above the Lac Saint Croix, about a 2 hour drive from Aix-en-Provence. This is a world renowned flying site for enthousiasts.

 

Where to go:

 

Saint Andre paraglyding club..

 

 

 

 

 

Horse riding

 

A number of riding schools are located across the region.

 

Where to go:

 

Centre Equestre - Les Crinieres enchantées, located between Lorgues and Salernes. For a 1 hour lesson expect to pay around 20 Euros.
