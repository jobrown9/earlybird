Things to do and places to visit in and around Provence

 

The Côte d'Azur

 

The whole coast offers a variety of activites and places to visit, historic towns such as Monte Carlo, Cannes, Saint Tropez, Antibes, Nice or Marseille offer everything you could every need as well as countless bars, cafes and old town centres to stroll around.

 

Hidden coves and beaches can be found all along the coast, as well as coastal paths ideal for biking or walking. Boundless activities are on offer, from snorkeling, diving, sailing, biking, trekking or simply discovering the towns and villages that line the coast.

 

 

 

The inner Provence region

 

Inland from the coast is where you can find numerous quintessentially Provençale villages, gently sloping landscapes, olive groves, lavender fields and vineyards. This is where you'll find local markets with all the local produce on offer as well as a chance to experience the authentic Provence.

 

There are a surprising number of activites on offer, from wine tasting, cannoing, cross country biking, paragliding or even horse riding. Although most people prefer to sit back and enjoy the sunshine and casually discover the villages and local attractions each village has to offer.
